I am a solutions architect based in Bengaluru. For the last nine years I have led architecture at Streamoid Technologies, where I work on two flagship platforms — an AI-native WebGL/WASM canvas for fashion design boards used by Puma, Ajio, and Aditya Birla Fashion & Retail, and a cloud-streamed Unreal Engine 3D studio that is part of the curriculum at the London College of Fashion.

Before Streamoid, I shipped Android at scale, hybrid positioning systems for alpine sports, SIP video calling, and a healthcare ASP.NET stack. One US patent on automated product video generation for fashion items.

On the side, I keep small open-source tools — four extensions in the VS Code Marketplace, a self-hosted web-search API, a Vue runtime for Google's A2UI protocol, and a UI library called Sable that does eight things on purpose. The Mogo family is personal research, not product.
