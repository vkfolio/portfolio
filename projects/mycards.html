MyCardsAndDocs exists because I wanted a place for ID cards and travel documents that did not ask me to make an account first. Cloud password managers do a great job at passwords. They do an awkward job at the rectangles.

what's on the device

  • cards, with templated fronts and an arbitrary back
  • documents — PDFs, photographs of paperwork, scans
  • QR / barcode pages for boarding passes & loyalty

what isn't

  • accounts. no sign-up screen at first launch
  • analytics. no SDKs at all
  • backup, by default. opt-in to iCloud Keychain if you want it
  • remote config. nothing the app does requires the network

build

SwiftUI · iOS 17+. ~2,800 lines of Swift; the largest single screen is the card scanner.
